- **impact-gate-scan-done.yml** (schedule: `*/5 * * * *`)

  - Auto-retry on hourly boundary (retry-errors at :00 mark)
  - Coverage tracking and data quality snapshots

### Scripts
- **scripts/impact_gate_polling_daemon.py** (12.9 KB)

- **scripts/scan_fix_issues_done.py** (15 KB)
  - Scans all done fix issues within lookback window
  - Identifies ungated issues and runs Impact Gate retroactively
  - Coverage percentage tracking
  - Generates alerts for insufficient coverage
