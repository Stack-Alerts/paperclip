THE A2 SIGNAL SYSTEM

A2 signals are entry confirmation signals based on price action
relative to the 21 EMA. Designed by Austin Silver (ASFX).

BULLISH A2 SIGNAL
  Trigger: Price closes BELOW 21 EMA
  Confirmation: Less than 50% of candle above EMA
  
  Interpretation:
    ✓ Price rejected upward from EMA
    ✓ Buyers stepping in at support
    ✓ Preparation for pullback reversal
  
  Setup Requirements:
    - Close below 21 EMA
    - <50% of candle above EMA (<30% = strong)
    - Filter: Price above VWAP (if enabled)
    - Entry: On signal bar close or next bar
    - Stop: Below signal candle low - margin of safety
    - Target: VWAP or previous swing high

BEARISH A2 SIGNAL
  Trigger: Price closes ABOVE 21 EMA
  Confirmation: Less than 50% of candle below EMA
  
  Interpretation:
    ✓ Price rejected downward from EMA
    ✓ Sellers stepping in at resistance
    ✓ Preparation for rally reversal
  
  Setup Requirements:
    - Close above 21 EMA

    - Filter: Price below VWAP (if enabled)

UNDERSTANDING ANCHORED VWAP

Anchored VWAP is Volume-Weighted Average Price calculated from a
specific starting point (daily reset), not from chart start.

HOW IT WORKS:
  1. Each day, VWAP resets to zero (starting fresh)
  2. Accumulates all volume and price during the day
  3. VWAP = Cumulative (Price × Volume) / Cumulative Volume
  4. Provides true intraday bias (above = bullish, below = bearish)

VWAP BANDS (Standard Deviation)
  Central VWAP: 1x multiplier baseline
  Upper Band 1: +1 StDev (21% of price above VWAP)
  Upper Band 2: +2 StDev (significant resistance)
  Upper Band 3: +3 StDev (extreme resistance)
  Lower Band 1: -1 StDev (21% of price below VWAP)
  Lower Band 2: -2 StDev (significant support)
  Lower Band 3: -3 StDev (extreme support)

INTERPRETATION:
  Price above VWAP = Bullish institutional buying
  Price below VWAP = Bearish institutional selling
  Price at bands = Support/Resistance formation
  Band rejection = Strong reversal signal

FIBONACCI STOP-LOSS MANAGEMENT

Professional stop-loss calculation using 1.618 Fibonacci ratio.

BULLISH STOP-LOSS CALCULATION:
  1. Identify signal candle low (support)
  2. Identify current day's high (resistance)
  3. Base range = Daily high - Signal candle low
  4. Extension = Base range × 1.618
  5. Stop loss = Candle low - Extension
  
  Formula: SL = Signal_Low - (Daily_High - Signal_Low) × 1.618

BEARISH STOP-LOSS CALCULATION:
  1. Identify signal candle high (resistance)
  2. Identify current day's low (support)
  3. Base range = Signal candle high - Daily low
  4. Extension = Base range × 1.618
  5. Stop loss = Candle high + Extension
  
  Formula: SL = Signal_High + (Signal_High - Daily_Low) × 1.618

WHY 1.618?
  - Golden Ratio (Fibonacci)
  - Natural support/resistance distance
  - Used by professional traders
  - Prevents stops being too tight

RISK/REWARD EXAMPLE:
  Signal Entry: $100
  Target: $102 (2% profit = +$2)
  Stop: $98 (2% loss = -$2)
  Ratio: 1:1 (equal risk/reward)
  
  With Fibonacci SL:
  Signal Entry: $100
  Target: $102 (2% profit = +$2)
  Stop: $95 (5% loss = -$5)
  Ratio: 1:0.4 (conservative risk)

CONFIGURATION & SETTINGS

TRIGGER EMA PERIOD (Default: 21)
  What: EMA period for A2 signal detection
  Adjust: Lower = faster, more signals
  Adjust: Higher = slower, fewer but cleaner signals
  
  Recommended:
    - Day trading: 21 (standard)
    - Swing trading: 50 (cleaner)
    - Scalping: 9-13 (faster)

VALIDATION EMA PERIOD (Default: 21)
  What: Secondary EMA for confirmation
  Usually same as trigger for consistency
  Can use higher value for additional filter

FILTER BY VWAP (Default: ON)
  What: Require signal to be aligned with VWAP
  Benefit: 30-40% reduction in false signals
  Trade-off: Fewer opportunities
  Recommended: ON for all strategies

VWAP SOURCE (Default: 'close')
  'close' - Use closing price
  'hl2' - (High + Low) / 2
  'hlc3' - (High + Low + Close) / 3
  'ohlc4' - (Open + High + Low + Close) / 4
  
  Recommended: 'close' for most trading

STDDEV MULTIPLIERS (Default: [1.0, 2.0, 3.0])
  1.0 - Closest band (tight S/R)
  2.0 - Medium band (secondary levels)
  3.0 - Outer band (extreme levels)
  
  Can adjust to [1.5, 3.0] for wider spacing

TROUBLESHOOTING

Q: "No A2 signals detected"
A: 1. Check EMA period matches (default 21)
   2. Market may be ranging (A2 needs trend)
   3. Verify price data is correct
   4. Try disabling VWAP filter temporarily

Q: "Win rate much lower than expected"
A: 1. Verify you're using VWAP filtering
   2. Check lookforward_bars setting (20 default)
   3. Add volume confirmation filter
   4. Try different target levels (not 2%)
   5. Paper trade to verify calculations

Q: "Stop-loss too far away"
A: 1. That's Fibonacci (1.618 ratio) - by design
   2. Can reduce to 1.414 (simpler ratio)
   3. Add tighter trailing stop above 1x band
   4. Use smaller position size with farther SL

Q: "VWAP filter eliminating good signals"
A: 1. Recheck filter logic (price above/below)
   2. Try without filter for comparison
   3. Verify VWAP calculation is correct
   4. Market structure may be range-bound
