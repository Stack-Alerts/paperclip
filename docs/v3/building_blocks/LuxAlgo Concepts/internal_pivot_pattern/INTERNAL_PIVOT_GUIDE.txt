WHY IS INTERNAL PIVOT PATTERN DIFFERENT?

TRADITIONAL PIVOT DETECTION:
  Requirement: Middle bar lowest (pivot low) or highest (pivot high)
  Looking Back: 21+ bars to confirm
  Confirmation: 1-3 additional bars after pivot forms
  Total Delay: 22-25 bars to confirm pivot
  Signal Lag: SLOW - You miss the initial move

INTERNAL PIVOT DETECTION:
  Requirement: Lower timeframe data within current candle
  Looking: At intrabar data (5-min, 15-min within daily bar)
  Confirmation: Immediate on candle close
  Total Delay: REAL-TIME - Signal on close of that bar
  Signal Speed: 70-80% FASTER - Catch pivots immediately

Example:
  Traditional: Takes 25 days to confirm daily pivot

THE PIVOT DETECTION MECHANISM

HOW INTERNAL PIVOTS ARE DETECTED:

1. LOWER TIMEFRAME ANALYSIS
   - Take current candle (e.g., daily bar)
   - Analyze 4x lower timeframe data (e.g., 4 × 4H = 1D)
   - Extract: opens, highs, lows, closes from intrabar

2. MIDDLE BAR TEST
   - Find middle bar within the intrabar data set
   - Check if it's the LOWEST (pivot low) or HIGHEST (pivot high)
   - Within the surrounding timeframe range

3. PIVOT LOW CONFIRMATION
   - Middle intrabar is lowest in range
   - Represents sellers exhausting
   - Buyers stepping in (reversal signal)
   - Color: BLUE (uptrend expected)

4. PIVOT HIGH CONFIRMATION
   - Middle intrabar is highest in range
   - Represents buyers exhausting
   - Sellers stepping in (reversal signal)
   - Color: ORANGE (downtrend expected)

PIVOT TYPES AND THEIR MEANINGS

PIVOT LOW (Blue Candles)
  Definition: Lowest point in intrabar structure
  Interpretation: Bullish reversal signal

PIVOT HIGH (Orange Candles)
  Definition: Highest point in intrabar structure
  Interpretation: Bearish reversal signal
  Market Behavior: Buyers rejected, sellers taking control

  Target: Next pivot low
  Stop: Above pivot high

ZIGZAG LINES
  What: Lines connecting sequential pivots
  Purpose: Visualize market structure
  Interpretation: Shows trend flow and reversals
  Use: Identify support/resistance levels

ACCURACY DASHBOARD

The Accuracy Dashboard measures how reliable your detected pivots are:

ACCURATE PIVOT LOW:
  Condition: Next detected pivot is HIGHER
  Meaning: Uptrend continued after reversal
  Indicator: ✓ Correct signal

INACCURATE PIVOT LOW:
  Condition: Next detected pivot is LOWER
  Meaning: Downtrend continued instead of reversing
  Indicator: ✗ False signal (move on)

ACCURATE PIVOT HIGH:
  Condition: Next detected pivot is LOWER
  Meaning: Downtrend continued after reversal
  Indicator: ✓ Correct signal

INACCURATE PIVOT HIGH:
  Condition: Next detected pivot is HIGHER
  Meaning: Uptrend continued instead of reversing
  Indicator: ✗ False signal (move on)

ACCURACY % CALCULATION:
  Accuracy = (Correct Pivots / Total Pivots) × 100
  
  Example:
    10 total pivots detected
    7 were followed by correct direction
    Accuracy = 70%

HOW TO USE ACCURACY:
  60%+: Good settings, proceed with trading
  50-60%: Acceptable, consider higher ratio
  <50%: Poor settings, lower timeframe ratio

TROUBLESHOOTING

Q: "No pivots detected"
A: 1. Data may have gaps (100+ bars minimum needed)
   2. Ratio too conservative (try higher)
   3. Lookback period too large (try 13 or 21)
   4. Check data quality (verify OHLC values)

Q: "Too many false signals"
A: 1. Lower timeframe ratio (ratio 2 or 3)
   2. Increase pivot lookback (21 or 34)
   3. Check accuracy dashboard (filter pivots <60%)
   4. Use larger timeframe (1H instead of 15min)

Q: "Accuracy below 60%"
A: 1. Try lower timeframe ratio
   2. Increase lookback period
   3. Different market conditions (ratio per asset)
   4. Test with TimeframeOptimizer

Q: "Zigzag lines seem disconnected"
A: 1. Ensure pivots alternate (low, high, low, high)
   2. Check for data gaps
   3. Verify lower timeframe data quality
   4. Visual check: should show smooth zigzag
