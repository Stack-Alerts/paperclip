# Pennant Pattern Building Block

**Block Number:** 44/66 | **Category:** Pattern-Based | **Version:** 1.0 | **Status:** ✅ Complete

## Overview
Short-term continuation pattern with strong "flagpole" followed by small symmetrical triangle consolidation.

## Technical Specifications
**Flagpole:** Sharp directional move (15-40% in short time)  
**Pennant Body:** Small symmetrical triangle (converging trendlines)  
**Duration:** Very short - typically 1-3 weeks maximum  
**File:** `src/detectors/building_blocks/patterns/pennant_pattern.py`

## Bitcoin Implementation
- High-probability continuation during Bitcoin momentum moves (65-75% win rate)
- Forms after strong impulse moves
- Pennant duration in Bitcoin: typically 1-2 weeks on daily charts
- Volume declining in pennant, spike on breakout
- Target equals flagpole length projected from breakout

## Trading Strategy
- Confirm strong flagpole with high volume (>2x average)
- Identify pennant formation (small converging triangle)
- Entry: Trade breakout direction with volume confirmation
- Stop: Opposite side of pennant
- Target: Flagpole height projected from breakout

## Confluence
- Pennant + strong trend = +20 points
- Quick pattern resolution = +15 points
